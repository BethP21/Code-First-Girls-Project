Hello, Welcome to Vegan Eats: London

Who are we?

This is a website run by a lover of vegan food! Our aim is to visit all of the fully vegan restaurants and cafes in London and review them for YOU! Hopefully the website will continually grow as we make our way around all of the restaurants. There are more and more restaurants opening in London which are fully vegan which is fab to see so we want to share what we think of them. We would love it if you could let us know of any restaurants you have visited or would like us to visit then we can feature them here.
